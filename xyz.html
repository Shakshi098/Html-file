Himachal Pradesh

Himachal Pradesh is situated in the heart of western Himalyas identify as "Dev Bhumi", meaning the land of god. This place abundant of natural beauty. Himachal Pradesh also known as the adobe of snow because the high snowy mountains and cold climate. Shimla is the capital of Himachal Pradesh. Himachal Pradesh has many beautiful towns and hamlets all over the state. The attractive place for tourists in Himachal Pradesh are Shimla, Manali, Kullu, Dhramshala etc.A large number of tourist visit the scared shrines of Himachal Pradesh through the year.
